Calibration Capabilities

Specialized metrology services accredited by SANAS for ionizing radiation and dimensional measurement traceability.

Ionizing Radiation Metrology

Comprehensive calibration of radiation monitoring equipment for medical, industrial, and environmental applications.

  • Gamma Radiation: Survey meters, dosimeters, and area monitors (Cs-137, Co-60)
  • X-Ray Calibration: Diagnostic radiology and industrial X-ray equipment
  • Dose Rate: Air kerma and ambient dose equivalent measurements
  • Personal Dosimetry: TLD and electronic personal dosimeters

Traceable Dimensional Metrology

Precision dimensional calibration traceable to national standards for manufacturing and quality control.

  • Length Standards: Gauge blocks, pin gauges, and ring gauges
  • Precision Instruments: Micrometers, calipers, and height gauges
  • Surface Plates: Granite and cast iron surface flatness
  • Thread Gauges: Plug and ring thread calibration

Did You Know?

South African law requires radiation monitoring equipment to be calibrated at least annually by a SANAS accredited laboratory to ensure compliance with the Hazardous Substances Act (Act 15 of 1973).
